## Creative Objective

## Target Persona

`Sleep-Deprived High Performer`

They want more stable daily function, but do not respond to loud optimization culture. They prefer calm structure, intelligent editing, and practical clarity.

`carousel`

- this topic needs sequence, not a one-line statement
- the content is more framework-led than atmosphere-led
- a five-slide carousel is enough to move from reframe to explanation to practical self-check
