## 1. Feed Reel

### Creative Objective

Reframe the viewer's default habit from "push through harder" to "pay attention to daily condition first."
Make Mitozz feel like a calm, intelligent brand that helps people think about their routine more clearly, not like a stimulant-led supplement ad.

### Target Persona

`Sleep-Deprived High Performer`

Primary viewer profile:

- busy professional
- mentally switched on but physically running flat
- tired of relying on adrenaline, caffeine, or pure willpower
- wants steadier daily condition without hype

### Format Decision

`reel`

Why:

- this topic works best as a quick behavioral reframe
- the viewer should recognize themselves in the first 1-2 seconds
- motion helps show the shift from strained push-through energy to calmer routine control

Resolved production system:

- `Template Set`: `Set 4 + Set 3`
- `Slide Blueprint`: `reel-4-shot`
- `asset_archetype`: `reel-source-frame`
- `locked_aspect_ratio`: `9:16`
- `locked_export_size`: `1536x2752`

### Creative Direction

- `core_concept`: before reaching for push-through mode, pause and reconsider what your daily condition is asking for
- `angle`: better function starts with steadier foundations, not louder stimulation
- `immediate_takeaway`: "This is not about trying harder. It is about paying attention to the condition underneath the day."
- `viewer_feeling`: recognized, lightly relieved, and curious
- `positioning`: premium, science-led, calm, useful
- `what_to_avoid`:
  - dramatic burnout acting
  - aggressive biohacking visuals
  - gym-performance energy
  - anything that implies instant recovery or medical effect

## 2. Same-Day Reinforcement Story

### Creative Objective

Support the reel with a lighter, Story-native reminder that busy days often get managed through force rather than condition awareness.
Drive taps to the reel while keeping the tone softer and more conversational than the feed asset.

### Target Persona

`Sleep-Deprived High Performer`

### Format Decision

`story sequence`

Why:

- this asset should reinforce and route, not repeat the full reel
- the job is clarity and recognition, not full explanation

Resolved production system:

- `Template Set`: `Set 1`
- `Slide Blueprint`: `story-3-frame`
- `story_delivery_mode`: `static-sequence`
- `story_aspect_ratio`: `9:16`

Why this delivery mode:

- the Story's main job is reinforcement and click-through
- static frames create better copy control and stronger readability than a reel recut here

### Creative Direction

- `core_concept`: a short three-step reminder that "pushing through" is not the only lens for a difficult day
- `angle`: lighten the mental load and invite reflection
- `immediate_takeaway`: "Maybe the issue is not effort. Maybe it is the condition underneath."
- `what_to_avoid`:
  - turning the Story into a mini-carousel
  - reusing the entire reel beat-for-beat
  - sounding preachy or self-help heavy
