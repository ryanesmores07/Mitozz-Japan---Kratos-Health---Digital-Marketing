# Mitozz Instagram Creative Design System

## Purpose

This document turns the approved `Steel Light System` into a repeatable production system for all Mitozz Instagram assets.

Use it when creating:

- feed carousels
- static feed posts
- stories
- reels
- authority posts
- product posts
- lifestyle posts

The goal is consistency without repetition.

## Core Principle

Every Mitozz creative should look like it belongs to the same brand world, but not like it came from the same exact file.

Keep fixed:

- palette
- typography logic
- whitespace discipline
- calm premium tone
- science-led restraint

Rotate:

- crop
- alignment
- image-to-text ratio
- focal subject
- background wash
- glow treatment
- lifestyle versus product emphasis

## Template Set Architecture

Use these five template sets as the primary system.

### Set 1. Education White Card

Use for:

- mitochondria explainers
- ingredient explainers
- myth clarifications
- routine education
- save-worthy carousel covers

Visual DNA:

- white or near-white editorial card
- mist-blue atmosphere
- subtle abstract science cue
- short Japanese headline
- strong hierarchy

Best asset types:

- carousel
- static education post
- story reinforcement

### Set 2. Editorial Science Layer

Use for:

- slightly deeper educational slides
- comparisons
- step-by-step frameworks
- concept breakdowns

Visual DNA:

- still light and minimal, but less card-like than Set 1
- layered whitespace zones
- thin dividers or soft highlight bars
- one supporting visual or abstract shape per frame
- more editorial offset layouts

Best asset types:

- mid-carousel slides
- longer educational posts
- FAQ slides

### Set 3. Luminous Product Hero

Use for:

- product introduction
- routine moments
- premium product focus
- softer conversion support
- end cards with product presence

Visual DNA:

- accurate Mitozz bottle fidelity
- cool-to-warm luminous atmosphere
- premium surface and backlight
- negative space for short copy
- no cluttered props
- use the locked bottle workflow when real package truth must stay exact

Best asset types:

- static feed post
- carousel cover
- last slide CTA
- reel source frame

### Set 4. Real-Life Ritual Lifestyle

Use for:

- morning routine
- desk, travel, or daily reset context
- calm real-life wellness scenes
- relatable lifestyle storytelling

Visual DNA:

- realistic photography or highly realistic renders
- natural light or diffused editorial daylight
- minimal environment styling
- believable premium everyday moments
- product can appear, but does not need to dominate

Best asset types:

- reel source frames
- lifestyle carousels
- story support assets
- bridge posts between education and product

### Set 5. Warm Trust Portrait

Use for:

- expert credibility
- founder or advisor style posts
- calm social proof
- trust-building posts

Visual DNA:

- quiet premium portraiture
- cloud-white and mist-blue base
- subtle warmth in skin tone and environment
- restrained text overlay
- composed, credible expression

Best asset types:

- static feed post
- carousel cover for authority content
- story Q&A

## Template Set Usage By Post Type

Educational carousel:

- cover: `Set 1`
- middle slides: `Set 1` plus `Set 2`
- end card: `Set 3` or simplified `Set 1`

Ingredient post:

- cover: `Set 1`
- explanation slides: `Set 2`
- final CTA: `Set 3`

Product post:

- hero frame: `Set 3`
- support slides: `Set 4` if lifestyle context helps

Lifestyle post:

- main mode: `Set 4`
- support typography slides: `Set 1` or `Set 2`

Expert or authority post:

- main mode: `Set 5`
- support educational slides: `Set 1`

Story sequence:

- opener: simplified `Set 1`, `Set 4`, or `Set 5`
- middle: simplified `Set 2`
- CTA frame: simplified `Set 3`

Reel:

- opening hook: `Set 4` or `Set 3`
- explanatory freeze or title frame: `Set 1`
- trust beat: `Set 5` when useful
- end frame: `Set 3`

## Carousel Adaptation Rules

The system must scale no matter how many slides a post has.

### 1-slide post

Use:

- one strong hook
- one focal subject
- one template set only

Best sets:

- `Set 1`
- `Set 3`
- `Set 5`

### 2 to 3 slides

Use:

- slide 1 for hook
- slide 2 for explanation or emotional payoff
- slide 3 for soft CTA when needed

Recommended structure:

- `Set 1` -> `Set 2` -> `Set 3`

### 4 to 6 slides

This is the default Mitozz carousel length.

Recommended structure:

1. hook
2. clarify
3. deepen
4. apply to daily life
5. reinforce trust or product relevance
6. soft CTA

Recommended template flow:

`Set 1` -> `Set 2` -> `Set 2` -> `Set 4` or `Set 1` -> `Set 3` or `Set 5` -> `Set 3`

### 7 to 10 slides

Use only when the topic truly needs more depth.

Rules:

- every slide must still hold one clear idea
- repeat the visual rhythm, not the exact layout
- insert a lighter slide every 2 to 3 frames to keep reading fatigue low

Recommended rhythm:

- slide 1: `Set 1`
- slide 2: `Set 2`
- slide 3: `Set 2`
- slide 4: `Set 4` or lighter `Set 1`
- slide 5: `Set 2`
- slide 6: `Set 2`
- slide 7: `Set 5` or `Set 4`
- slide 8 to 10: `Set 3` or simplified `Set 1` for CTA and recap

## Slide Role System

Regardless of slide count, assign each frame one of these roles:

- `Hook`
- `Definition`
- `Clarification`
- `Daily-life translation`
- `Proof or trust`
- `Product relevance`
- `Soft CTA`

This makes the design system easier to scale and keeps carousels from feeling random.

## Lifestyle And Realistic Image Rules

Lifestyle and realistic imagery are encouraged, but they must still feel premium and believable.

Always use:

- natural daylight or diffused studio daylight
- quiet spaces
- premium everyday styling
- realistic skin texture
- genuine objects and environments
- minimal prop count

Good scene directions:

- morning light at a clean table
- calm desk setup
- quiet travel pouch or bag moment
- soft mirror or sink-side ritual
- reading, journaling, or reset moment

Avoid:

- staged influencer poses
- hyper-luxury sets that feel fake
- crowded kitchens or busy homes
- overly dramatic fitness scenes
- glossy commercial prop piles

## Text Density Rules

Low-density slide:

- 3 to 8 words
- strongest visual slide

Medium-density slide:

- 8 to 18 words
- standard Mitozz education frame

High-density slide:

- up to 28 words
- use sparingly and only with `Set 2`

If a slide needs more than that, split it into two frames.

## Production Rules For Prompt Writing

When building prompts, always specify:

- which `template_set` the asset uses
- which slide role the frame serves
- whether the frame is text-led, product-led, portrait-led, or lifestyle-led
- what must stay fixed from the brand system
- what can vary for freshness

Reference priority:

1. approved working examples
2. style anchors
3. product-source images for bottle fidelity

## Approval Checklist

Approve only if the asset:

- clearly fits one template set
- still feels recognizably Mitozz
- reads well on mobile
- keeps one idea per frame
- feels premium without trying too hard
- uses realistic imagery when lifestyle is present
- adds variation without drifting from the system

## Recommended Default System

If the team wants a default setup for most monthly content:

- `Set 1` for 45 percent of assets
- `Set 2` for 20 percent of assets
- `Set 3` for 15 percent of assets
- `Set 4` for 10 percent of assets
- `Set 5` for 10 percent of assets

This keeps the account education-first while giving enough room for product, lifestyle, and trust content.
