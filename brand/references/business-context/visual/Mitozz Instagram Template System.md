# Mitozz Instagram Template System

## Purpose

This template system gives Mitozz Japan a consistent Instagram design language that supports the real goal of the account:

- build trust before selling
- make science feel clear and premium
- create a recognizable feed without becoming repetitive
- keep the brand distinct from generic supplement marketing

This is not one rigid layout. It is one premium system with fixed brand DNA and controlled variation.

## Template Name

`Steel Light System`

Shorthand:

Soft science, quiet confidence, premium clarity.

## Strategic Role

The Instagram account should feel education-first, medically responsible, and visually elevated.

What the audience should feel:

- `This brand looks intelligent and calm.`
- `This feels more premium than typical wellness content.`
- `I can trust this without feeling sold to.`
- `The posts are beautiful enough to save, but still easy to understand.`

## Fixed Brand DNA

These elements should stay consistent across almost every asset:

- palette built around Cloud White, Mist Blue, Steel Blue, and restrained Apricot Glow
- large amounts of whitespace
- minimal composition with one clear message per frame
- clean Japanese-premium typography led by `Noto Sans JP`
- editorial restraint with short English accents only when useful
- soft, luminous lighting
- calm, touchable surfaces and frosted atmosphere
- educational, trust-first tone rather than hard conversion

## Creative Guardrails

Always do:

- make the post feel save-worthy before making it feel promotional
- keep the layout breathable on mobile
- let the brand feel premium through restraint
- show science through atmosphere and structure, not through loud lab visuals
- keep product and people polished but natural

Never do:

- neon biotech styling
- dark performance-marketing aesthetics
- crowded infographic design
- flashy supplement-ad compositions
- exaggerated medical or anti-aging claims
- trendy graphic clutter that weakens trust

## Core Template Family

Use these as the three primary feed modes.

### 1. Education White Card

Primary role:

- foundational education
- ingredient explainers
- myth-vs-clarity posts
- routine education

Visual structure:

- 4:5 vertical
- white or near-white card-led composition
- soft mist-blue atmospheric wash behind or around the card
- one headline and one idea per slide
- subtle abstract science cue only

Design behavior:

- dominant whitespace
- centered or slightly offset card
- text hierarchy first, image second
- apricot used only as a small signal

Best for:

- carousel covers
- save-worthy explainers
- series posts that build brand authority

### 2. Luminous Product Hero

Primary role:

- product familiarity
- ritual positioning
- premium pack presence
- softer conversion support

Visual structure:

- 4:5 vertical
- bottle occupies roughly 25 to 45 percent of the frame
- luminous cool-to-warm gradient atmosphere
- soft backlight, subtle reflection, clean surface
- extra negative space for short copy or no copy

Design behavior:

- product floats or sits in a restrained premium environment
- label and cap stay faithful to approved source images
- warm apricot accent can be slightly stronger here than in education posts

Best for:

- hero posts
- ritual reminders
- bridge posts between education and trust

### 3. Warm Trust Portrait

Primary role:

- expert trust
- calm human relatability
- authority without looking corporate

Visual structure:

- 4:5 vertical
- warm but still airy framing
- cloud-white and mist-blue base with skin-friendly warmth
- calm expression, quiet styling, minimal distractions

Design behavior:

- portrait or upper-body crop
- copy stays short and secondary
- the person should feel credible, composed, and non-performative

Best for:

- expert-led posts
- testimonial-style authority cards
- trust-building content between science posts

## Story Companion Mode

Stories should be part of the same system, but lighter.

Rules:

- 9:16 vertical
- very generous top and bottom safe areas
- less text than feed
- same palette, same calm tone, same restraint
- built for reinforcement, polls, FAQ, and low-pressure engagement

Preferred structure:

- frame 1: one clear hook
- frame 2: one clarifying line
- frame 3: one soft CTA

## Recommended Grid Rhythm

Use a repeating rhythm instead of repeating one composition:

1. `Education White Card`
2. `Luminous Product Hero`
3. `Warm Trust Portrait`
4. `Education White Card`
5. `Luminous Product Hero`
6. `Education White Card`
7. `Warm Trust Portrait`
8. `Luminous Product Hero`
9. `Soft result or human-led premium lifestyle image`

This keeps the brand recognizable while making the grid feel intentional and premium.

## Copy System

On-canvas copy should feel:

- intelligent
- calm
- concise
- elegant
- non-salesy

Working copy rules:

- lead with one strong hook
- avoid long paragraphs
- make Japanese copy the main communication layer
- use English only as a small editorial accent
- end with soft CTAs like `保存してあとで見返す`, `やさしく理解する`, or `詳しく見る`

## Uniqueness System

To keep the template consistent but not repetitive, rotate only these elements:

- card alignment: centered, left-offset, right-offset
- camera distance
- crop ratio inside the 4:5 frame
- glow intensity
- abstract cue type
- product versus text dominance
- portrait versus object balance
- apricot accent placement

Do not rotate the core atmosphere, restraint, or typography logic.

## Template Usage By Content Pillar

`Mitochondria, Explained Simply`

`Epicatechin and the Science Story`

- default template: `Education White Card`
- alternate support template: `Luminous Product Hero`

`Better Function, Not Hype`

- default template: `Warm Trust Portrait` or calm `Reel`
- support template: `Education White Card`

`Daily ritual and product use`

- default template: `Luminous Product Hero`

`Expert validation`

- default template: `Warm Trust Portrait`

## Premium Execution Checklist

Before approving any asset, confirm:

- it feels recognizably Mitozz even without a logo
- it is cleaner than a typical wellness post
- it looks expensive because of restraint, not because of effects
- the science feels approachable
- the mobile reading experience is easy
- it fits one of the approved template modes
- it adds variation without breaking the system

## Final Recommendation

Use `Steel Light System` as the permanent Mitozz Instagram template family.

If the team needs one default starting point, start with:

- `Education White Card` for 70 percent of educational feed content
- `Luminous Product Hero` for 20 percent of feed content
- `Warm Trust Portrait` for 10 percent of feed content

That mix matches the current project goal best: premium education-led growth with trust, consistency, and visual distinction.
