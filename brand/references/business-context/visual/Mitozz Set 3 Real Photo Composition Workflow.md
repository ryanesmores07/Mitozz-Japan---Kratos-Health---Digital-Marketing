# Mitozz Set 3 Real Photo Composition Workflow

## Purpose

This is the approved production workflow for `Set 3: Luminous Product Hero` when exact bottle fidelity matters.

The bottle should not be AI-redrawn.

Instead:

- use the real bottle photo as the visible product
- generate or design the background environment separately
- composite the two together with realistic lighting and surface integration

## Production Steps

### 1. Choose the real bottle image

Use the approved bottle image as the product truth.

Do not:

- repaint the bottle
- rebuild the label
- recolor the package
- ask AI to recreate it from scratch

### 2. Generate a background plate

Generate a bottle-free product-hero background plate with:

- cloud-white and mist-blue atmosphere
- restrained apricot warmth
- believable premium tabletop or surface
- soft backlight
- generous negative space for headline
- a clear bottle placement zone

The plate should be beautiful on its own, but intentionally leave room for the real bottle.

### 3. Composite the real bottle into the plate

Place the real bottle into the bottle placement zone.

Adjust only what is necessary:

- scale
- position
- local brightness
- local warmth or coolness
- contact shadow
- surface reflection strength

Do not distort package identity.

### 4. Add copy last

After the bottle and plate feel integrated:

- add the headline
- add the subline if needed
- keep typography minimal

## Composition Rules

For the final frame:

- bottle occupies around 25 to 40 percent of the frame
- one main negative-space area remains available for text
- bottle sits on a believable surface
- camera height feels natural and premium
- the product remains the clearest focal element

## Realism Checks

Before approval, confirm:

- bottle perspective matches the plate
- highlight direction on the bottle matches the environment
- contact shadow is present and believable
- bottle does not appear cut out or floating
- reflection intensity matches the surface material
- color temperature across bottle and plate feels unified

## Fallback Rule

If a generated product hero keeps drifting the bottle design, do not keep refining the AI bottle render.

Return to this workflow and use:

- real bottle photo
- generated background plate
- manual composition

That is the approved Set 3 production method.
