6

Instagram Metrics Snapshot

Based on the Instagram Insights screenshot captured on 2026-03-31, the account generated 132 total views, reached 106 accounts, drove 17 profile visits, and reached 1 total follower by month end.

The strongest early signal is discovery: 83.3% of views came from non-followers, and reels drove 87.1% of all views. That suggests the account is starting to get visibility beyond its existing audience, with reels acting as the main reach format so far.

The main limitation is that engagement and conversion are still very early. The account recorded 2 total interactions and only 1 follower, which means visibility has started, but stronger repetition, trust, and follow conversion are still ahead.

The strongest content shown in the snapshot was the 2026-03-25 reel with 108 views, far ahead of the other March posts. Overall, the March numbers are modest but useful: Mitozz Japan is beginning to get non-follower reach and profile visits, which is the right early signal for a new account.
