# March 2026 Monthly Report

Brand: Mitozz Japan

## Overview

March focused on building the strategic foundation for Mitozz Japan and turning that into consistent organic Instagram execution. Alongside the core brand and research documents created for Mitozz Japan, we began executing the Instagram content system from March 23 onward, carried that momentum into the first week of April creative, and started the first stage of Shopify lifecycle marketing through native discount codes and email-capture popups.

- Foundational Mitozz Japan documents were created to support clearer long-term direction, including brand voice, strategy, audience research, and positioning work.

### 2. Organic Instagram Planning And Execution

- The Mitozz Japan content calendar was planned and maintained through April.

### 3. Creative Direction And Design Rules

- Alongside the creatives themselves, we also established clearer design and style rules for how Mitozz Japan should look and feel on Instagram.
- This included defining a more premium, calm, and trustworthy visual direction so the brand presents consistently across feed posts, Stories, and reels.

- The goal of organic Instagram for Mitozz Japan is not simply to maximize follower count quickly.

- This means the content direction is intentionally focused on education, reassurance, and premium brand perception instead of chasing broad vanity metrics.

### 5. Retention And Client-Facing Progress

- Mitozz Japan entered April with a clearer brand foundation, a working content plan, and active creative execution already underway.
- The account now has stronger consistency between strategy, messaging, and what is being posted publicly.

## Instagram Metrics Snapshot

Based on the Instagram Insights screenshot captured on `2026-03-31`, the account generated `132` total views, reached `106` accounts, drove `17` profile visits, and reached `1` total follower by month end.

The strongest positive signal is that `83.3%` of views came from non-followers, which shows the account is beginning to get discovery outside its existing audience. Reels also drove `87.1%` of all views, making them the clearest early reach format in the current content mix.

The main limitation is that engagement and conversion are still at a very early stage. The account recorded `2` total interactions and only `1` follower, which means visibility has started, but repeated familiarity and stronger conversion into follows are not there yet.

The top-performing content by views was the `2026-03-25` reel with `108` views, far ahead of the other March posts shown in the screenshot. This suggests that short-form video is currently doing most of the account's discovery work, while the rest of the content system is still in the earliest testing phase.

Overall, the March numbers are modest, but directionally useful: Mitozz is beginning to get non-follower reach and profile visits, which is the right first signal for a new account, even though follower growth and engagement remain very limited so far.
