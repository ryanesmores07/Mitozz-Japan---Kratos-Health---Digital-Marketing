# Business Context

Put long-lived brand and business reference files here.

## Recommended Use

- `visual/`: Brand visual direction, moodboards, example posts, art direction files.
- `visual/reference-pack/`: Official image-reference pack for Nano Banana generation, split into style anchors and approved working examples.
- `strategy/`: Positioning, messaging, offers, business strategy, brand narrative.
- `content-planning/`: Calendars, campaign plans, content matrices, topic schedules.
- `reporting/`: Retainer action logs, monthly summaries for Jay, and reporting templates.

When generating or refining Instagram image prompts, prioritize the most relevant file in these folders before making new creative assumptions.
