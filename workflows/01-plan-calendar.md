# 01. Plan Calendar

Use this checklist when shaping or revising the monthly Instagram calendar.

## Inputs

- audience and persona notes
- brand strategy
- brand voice
- `brand/references/business-context/visual/Brand Visual Direction.md`
- latest content calendar

## Checklist

- Confirm the month's strategic goal.
- If the account is in launch phase or near zero followers, bias the month toward discovery:
  - `3` feed posts per week
  - at least `1` Reel per week
  - Stories on feed/Reel days by default
  - at most `1` extra standalone Story day per week
- Balance education, trust, and product-support content.
- Rotate `asset_archetype` across the plan:
  - `education-card`
  - `product-hero`
  - `portrait-trust`
  - `story-reinforcement`
- Avoid repeating the same dominant feed composition in adjacent posts unless it is an intentional campaign sequence.
- Flag which posts should likely need product visuals, human portraits, or text-first layouts.
- Leave enough spacing for save-worthy educational posts and lighter reinforcement stories.
- Keep template variation inside the same Mitozz brand world so the full grid stays cohesive.
- Leave template resolution to the central mapping config instead of adding production columns to the calendar.
- Use `brand/references/business-context/content-planning/Mitozz Launch Phase Posting Strategy.md` when planning for a zero-follower or near-zero-follower account.

## Output

- a dated content calendar row or revision
- a rough archetype suggestion for each planned asset
- use one shared table for feed and story rows
- keep client-facing planning fields in Japanese
- keep workflow control fields stable and machine-friendly
- keep detailed production decisions in the post-calendar layer described in `workflows/03-post-calendar-production-flow.md`
