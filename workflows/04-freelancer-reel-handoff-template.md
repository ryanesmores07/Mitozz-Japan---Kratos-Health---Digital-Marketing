## Runtime Standard

- Hard maximum: `60 seconds`
- Best-practice default: `8-15 seconds`
- Recommended ranges:
  - `hook / awareness`: `8-10 seconds`
  - `education / explainer`: `10-15 seconds`
  - `routine / lifestyle`: `8-12 seconds`
  - `product / trust`: `8-12 seconds`
  - `deep-dive`: `15-30 seconds` only if the concept earns it
- Do not use `30-60 seconds` unless the reel has unusually strong narrative clarity and retention potential
- The first `1-2 seconds` must deliver the hook
- Keep the ending concise; do not let the CTA drag

## Brand And Tone Rules

The edit must feel:

- premium
- trustworthy
- calm
- modern Japanese wellness
- science-led without feeling clinical or cold
- educational before promotional

The edit must not feel:

- loud
- trend-chasing
- gimmicky
- flashy supplement advertising
- hyperactive
- overly cute
- heavily medical

## Messaging Rules

- Keep messaging clear and easy to understand at mobile speed.
- Prioritize one main message per beat.
- Do not turn the reel into a claim-heavy sales ad.
- Avoid direct medical, anti-aging, guaranteed-outcome, or fatigue-recovery claims.
- Preserve the approved Japanese copy exactly unless a revision is explicitly requested.
- If text must be shortened for timing, keep the meaning intact and reduce length, not sophistication.

## Design Rules

- Use the approved Mitozz visual system only.
- Keep the Steel Light palette behavior: cloud-white, mist-blue, restrained warmth.
- Preserve clean whitespace and premium spacing.
- Favor elegant editorial motion over aggressive animation.
- Use typography that feels modern, calm, and premium.
- Keep text readable on mobile without looking oversized or cheap.
- If the reel includes bottle or product shots, preserve pack fidelity and label orientation.

## Non-Negotiables

- Use only the provided source assets unless new assets are explicitly requested.
- Do not rewrite approved messaging.
- Do not introduce off-brand fonts, colors, stickers, or effects.
- Do not over-edit the reel into a generic social ad.
- Do not distort the product, label, or package appearance.
- Keep the final tone premium, calm, and trustworthy.

## Revision Policy

The first freelancer draft should be reviewed against:

- hook strength
- pacing
- clarity of message
- premium brand fit
- readability
- source-asset accuracy
- CTA effectiveness

Use revisions for refinement, not for changing the strategy after the fact.
